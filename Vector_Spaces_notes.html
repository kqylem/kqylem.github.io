Deﬁnition (Order of a group) the order of a group G is the number of el-
ements contained in G, denoted |G|. If a group is ﬁnite, then it is of ﬁnite order.
Deﬁnition (Abelian group) a group is called abelian if it is commutative,
that is, it satisﬁes a ∗ b = b ∗ a ∀a, b ∈ G.
