Let W be a subspace of a vector space V . Suppose that β is a basis for W .
Then β ⊆ V , and β is necessarily a linearly independent subset of V . Hence
by Theorem 1.13, there exists a maximal linearly independent subset of V that
contains β. We know from Theorem 1.12 that this maximal linearly indepen-
dent subset of V is a basis for V , and hence β is contained in a basis for V as
desired. ■
